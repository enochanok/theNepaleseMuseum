About TNM

The Nepalese Museum stands as a cultural institution dedicated to preserving and showcasing the rich heritage and history of Nepal. Located in the heart of the capital city, Kathmandu, this esteemed museum serves as a gateway to the country's diverse cultural traditions, art, history, and natural beauty. With its compelling mission and vision, the museum aims to engage visitors, both local and international, in an immersive journey through Nepal's past, present, and future.
